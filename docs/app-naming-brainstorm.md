# App Naming Brainstorm

**Date**: 26 January 2026
**Status**: Decision made
**Final Choice**: **Pharos**

---

## Context

The working title "Playout Analytics" was too generic and corporate-sounding. Needed something that:
- Appeals to MMM (Marketing Mix Modelling) & econometricians
- Is memorable and professional
- Reflects the navigation/discovery aspect of exploring campaign data
- Connects to Route's internal "Project Magellan" (main research project)

## Final Decision

### **Pharos**

**Why it works:**
- **The Lighthouse of Alexandria** - One of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World
- **Perfect metaphor** - A guiding light through campaign data, helps you navigate safely
- **Greek heritage** - Connects to ancient navigation tradition
- **Distinctive** - No common UK brand associations
- **Clean** - No unfortunate double meanings or product conflicts
- **Visual potential** - Lighthouse silhouette ✓ (implemented with Icons8 icon)

**The metaphor:**
> The Pharos of Alexandria was the ancient world's great lighthouse - a beacon that guided ships safely to harbour. This tool is your lighthouse through OOH campaign data.

**The family:**
| Project | Name | Role |
|---------|------|------|
| Main research | **Magellan** | The expedition |
| Econometrics tool | **Pharos** | The guiding light |
