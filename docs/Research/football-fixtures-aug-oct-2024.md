## Key Observations for Footfall Analysis

1. **29 Sep (top spike)**: Man United vs Spurs at Old Trafford -- massive draw, 73k+ capacity. Also Europa League MD1 was 4 days earlier (25 Sep, also Old Trafford).

2. **Saturdays with most PL HOME fixtures**: 21 Sep (8 home games), 14 Sep (8), 28 Sep (7), 5 Oct (7), 31 Aug (7), 17 Aug (6), 24 Aug (6).

3. **London density**: On a typical PL Saturday, 3-4 London clubs play at home simultaneously (Arsenal, Chelsea, Spurs, West Ham, Crystal Palace, Fulham, Brentford rotate home/away).

4. **Midweek European nights** (Tue/Wed/Thu): Generate footfall spikes on non-standard days. Key dates: 17-18 Sep (CL MD1 + Carabao R3), 24-25 Sep (Carabao R3 + EL MD1), 1-2 Oct (CL MD2).

5. **Bank Holiday Monday 26 Aug**: Aston Villa vs Arsenal (Villa Park, Birmingham) -- unusual Monday PL match + end of bank holiday weekend.

6. **International breaks**: Sat 7 Sep and Sat 12 Oct show as spike dates despite NO domestic football -- suggests non-football causes (shopping, events, bank holiday proximity for 7 Sep being start of September break).
